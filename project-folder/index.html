About Decarbify

Decarbify is a platform designed to help you calculate the carbon footprint of your devices and offer recommendations on reducing your carbon emissions. Our goal is to make sustainability accessible and actionable for everyone.

Features

Accurate Calculations

Get precise calculations of your device's carbon footprint based on real data.

Personalized Recommendations

Receive tailored suggestions to help you reduce your carbon emissions effectively.

Track Progress

Monitor your improvements over time and see the positive impact of your actions.
